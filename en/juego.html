How To Play

Welcome to Hangman Souls! You control the character on the left and you have to defeat the enemies. You have to discover the word letter per letter. But beware! If you fail the enemy will attack you and you will lose life points.

The enemy has no life bar, but that does not mean that he is immortal: to defeat him you have to guess the entire word. Always look at the word hint, and think about what the word is. If you write wrong letters, you can see them in the wrong letters sectiion and not make the mistake again.
